What is Affiliate Hub Pro?

Affiliate Hub Pro is a free browser-based affiliate marketing simulator that lets you practice offer selection, traffic channel strategy, and monetization decisions without using real money.

Who is this affiliate marketing simulator for?

It is designed for beginners, creators, and affiliate operators who want hands-on practice with SEO, email, community marketing, and paid traffic testing.

Can I learn affiliate marketing without paying for ads?

Yes. The simulator includes organic traffic paths such as search content, email, and community so users can understand affiliate economics without relying on ad spend.

What metrics should beginners watch first?

Start with click-through rate, conversion rate, EPC, refund risk, and payout timing. Those numbers reveal whether an affiliate campaign is improving or just looks busy.

Is this affiliate marketing simulator free to use?

Yes, Affiliate Hub Pro is completely free. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. It runs entirely in your browser with no downloads required.

How does the affiliate marketing game work?

Select an affiliate offer, choose a traffic channel like SEO content, email, or short video, then use your daily actions to publish, research, or build trust. End the day to simulate results and watch your campaigns compound over time.

Can I play this affiliate simulator on mobile?

Absolutely. The simulator is fully optimized for mobile devices with touch-friendly controls, swipeable carousels, and a bottom action dock for easy one-handed play. Add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

What is EPC in affiliate marketing?

EPC stands for Earnings Per Click. It measures how much revenue you earn for each click sent to an offer. Higher EPC means you can afford to spend more on traffic while staying profitable.

How do I get better at affiliate marketing using this simulator?

Practice daily. The simulator rewards consistency with login bonuses and streak rewards. Focus on matching offers to the right channels, building trust over time, and reinvesting profits into scaling what works.
